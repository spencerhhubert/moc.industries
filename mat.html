Builder's Mat

A hobby mat designed for LEGO builders

The 45x30cm (~17.7x11.8") mat is covered in a grid split acting as a two dimensional ruler measured in LEGO units. The left side's design represents plates or bricks stacked on top each other from a profile view, and the right side of the mat shows studs from a top-down point of view.

The Builder's Mat also has the angles for common slopes and wedges measured against those two grids, along with quarter circles at various radii, which can be used to quickly get the right angle or curve when constructing a large assembly.

Finally the mat is complete with some common LEGO brick math, showing the many patterns in the dimenisons of LEGO bricks with a QR code to more tips.
